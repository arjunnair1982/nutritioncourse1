FAQ

How much time does it take?

Plan for 5–10 minutes a day, plus an optional 60-minute live Q&A each week.

Is this suitable if I’m managing diabetes or heart risk?

Yes. We focus on evidence-based patterns (DASH, Mediterranean) shown to support cardiometabolic health. Always consult your clinician for personalized advice.

Will I get personalized feedback?

Guided and VIP tiers include weekly Q&A and tailored feedback. VIP adds a private consult.

Do you provide meal plans?

You’ll create a personalized, flexible plan in Week 4. We also provide sample plans and a recipe bank for inspiration.
